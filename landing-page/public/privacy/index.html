Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

At LogicCanvas, we believe your work, sketches, and algorithmic ideas are entirely your own. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in our mobile applications and website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

LogicCanvas does not collect, transmit, or store any of your custom whiteboard drawings, code snippets, notes, or data structures. All sketches you create remain locally sandboxed on your device.

2. iCloud Synchronization

If you choose to enable iCloud Sync, your whiteboard states are backed up and synced via Apple's secure iCloud APIs. This data is saved in your private iCloud storage container. asdevify has no access, visibility, or control over your iCloud files.

3. In-App Subscriptions

LogicCanvas Pro purchases are handled by Apple's App Store payment systems and validated anonymously via RevenueCat. We do not store or process any financial, credit card, or personal user identity credentials on our servers.

4. On-Device AI Models

Our smart shape detection algorithms and handwriting recognition machine learning models execute entirely on-device. Your drawings, pen movements, and text translations are never uploaded to third-party servers.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at: privacy@asdevify.com.
